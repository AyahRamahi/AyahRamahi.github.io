Projects

Quotr

A web application made with Flask. Every time you open the website or refresh the main page you get a random quote with the name of the author and name of the user who added this quote to the website's PostgreSQL database from books, songs, or movies. You can sign up and login to your account and see your quotes that were already added and add more or delete some if you want.

A-Read

A web application made with Flask. In this website you can register for a new account and login. You can look for a name of a book in the database of the website, go to the page of the book and a review for the book and also you can rate it. In the page of the book you can see the average rate of the book and other users reviews of it.

Activities

ACM Problem Solving Contests

I was qualified twice to the regional ACM ACPC contest in Egypt after participating in a national contest for all teams in Jordan. In 2018, my team got the 8th place from more than 80teams participating from all Jordan. In this contest we participate as teams of 3 and we use programming languages like C++ to solve problems given, teams who solve more problems with less time rank higher.


Youth Leadership Development Program

After a whole week of assessment , where we were being watched by mentors and engaged in several activities , I was one of the 30 students that were qualified for the training from all the 70 students that entered the assessment week. Then we had a 2 week training on leadership were we had challenges everyday and we had to solve them with our teams , choosing a different team leader each time. This program was given to us by Insperational Development Group in association with Sandhurst Academy, and was sponsered by King Abdullah II Fund For Development.


Translation Volunteer
