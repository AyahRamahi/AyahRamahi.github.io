ACM Competitions Participation

Our team was qualified with 15 other teams from Jordan from more than 60 that participated in the contest. Youth Leadership Development Program

After a whole week of assessment , where we were being watched by mentors and engaged in several activities , I was one of the 30 students that were qualified for the training from all the 70 students that entered the assessment week. Then we had a 2 week training on leadership were we had challenges everyday and we had to solve them with our teams , choosing a different team leader each time. This program was given to us by Insperational Development Group in association with Sandhurst Academy, and was sponsered by King Abdullah II Fund For Development.
